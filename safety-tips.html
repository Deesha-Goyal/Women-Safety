Smart Safety Tips

1. Create a secret code word or emoji that you can text or say to someone if you're in danger.
2. Use apps that trigger fake incoming calls at set times.
3. Keep pepper spray, but also remember — hairspray to the eye is equally dramatic!
4. Keep keychain-size devices that emit extremely loud sounds when triggered.
5. Always share your live location — because your bestie is basically Sherlock Holmes!
6. Keep your phone charged. You can’t throw it at anyone if it's dead.
7. Try not to stick to the same daily routes or routines - switch up your walking paths occassionally to avoid stalking.
